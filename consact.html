Solutions to Shark Endangerment

Many species of Sharks are becoming endangered due to climate change, being killed by humans, and other factors. To combat Shark Endangerment due to Climate Change, laws and policies that aim to prevent climate change should be passed and promoted. Laws and regulations must also be passed to prevent Sharks from being killed by Humans.

The Shark Conservation Act of 2010

The Shark Conservation Act of 2010 was passed with unanimous approval from both chambers of the US Congress in December 2010. It marked a giant step forward in the initiative to protect endangered Sharks from extinction. This law goes a long way toward protecting sharks, who are especially vulnerable due to the few amount of offspring they produce.
