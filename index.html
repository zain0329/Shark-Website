Description

This is a website created to inform people about Sharks. Sharks face many problems such as fin piracy and other problems that often leave them deceased. Human and natural activity has caused many shark species to become endangered. This website wil also explore solutions to problems that cause Sharks to become endangered.

Zain Malik Period 4, May 2021
